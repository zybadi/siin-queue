📥 BOOKMARKLET SETUP

The bookmarklet lets you push orders from hub.siin.shop into this queue with one click — no copy-pasting. Here's how to set it up:

1
Show your browser's bookmarks bar if it's hidden:
Chrome / Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+B (or Cmd+Shift+B on Mac)
2
Drag this button into your bookmarks bar:
📥 Push to Queue
↑ drag this into your bookmarks bar
3
During your stream, open hub.siin.shop in another tab. When you want to update the queue, refresh that tab, then click "📥 Push to Queue" in your bookmarks bar. New orders will appear here instantly with a sound alert.
4
Keep this queue tab open at all times. The bookmarklet sends data directly to it using the browser's built-in messaging.

⚠ If the bookmarklet doesn't detect orders

Hub.siin.shop may use a structure the bookmarklet doesn't recognize yet. In that case, just use the manual Add form above — you type the customer name + item from the chat or orders page. It takes about 3 seconds per order and works perfectly.
